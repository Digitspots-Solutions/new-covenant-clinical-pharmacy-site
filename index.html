About Us

New Covenant Clinical Pharmacy is a leading pharmaceutical manufacturing company proudly located in Obehie ASA, Ukwa West LGA, Abia State. We specialize in the production of high-quality pharmaceutical products that meet international standards while serving the healthcare needs of Nigerians. Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern manufacturing technology with rigorous quality control processes to ensure that every product leaving our premises is safe, effective, and reliable.

With a commitment to excellence and a deep understanding of the Nigerian healthcare landscape, we are dedicated to improving lives through accessible, affordable, and superior pharmaceutical solutions. Our team of qualified professionals works tirelessly to maintain the highest standards of pharmaceutical manufacturing, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and industry best practices.

Our Quality Standards

At New Covenant Clinical Pharmacy, quality is not just a promise—it's our foundation. We adhere to Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) guidelines and maintain NAFDAC certification for all our pharmaceutical products. Our quality management system encompasses every stage of production, from raw material procurement to final product testing.

We invest continuously in training our personnel, upgrading our equipment, and implementing the latest quality control technologies. Every product undergoes rigorous testing in our on-site laboratory before distribution. We maintain complete traceability for all our products and implement strict documentation procedures. Our commitment to quality ensures that healthcare providers and patients across Nigeria can trust our products with confidence.
